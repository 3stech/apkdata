Privacy Policy
Effective date: 1 May 2026  ·  Developer: 3S Technologies
ThithiAlert is a Tamil Hindu panchangam and reminder app. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights. We have designed the app with privacy in mind — it does not collect or store any personally identifiable information on our servers.

1. Information we do not collect

ThithiAlert does not collect, transmit, or store on any external server:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact details
  • The text or labels of reminders you create
  • Your precise GPS location or coordinates
  • Any financial or payment card information
  • Any content you enter in the Feedback form beyond what you voluntarily send to our support email

2. Information stored on your device

All reminders, tradition preferences, language settings, and calendar configuration you create within the app are stored locally on your Android device using the app's private database. This data never leaves your device except as part of a standard Android backup if you have enabled Google Drive backup on your device.

3. Analytics and crash reporting — Firebase

ThithiAlert uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, services provided by Google LLC, to help us understand how the app is used and to fix crashes.

Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • App events such as screen views, feature usage, and subscription actions
  • A randomly generated device-level identifier (not linked to your name or contact details)
  • General device information: Android version, device model, app version
  • Your approximate country or region (not precise location)
We have configured Firebase Analytics to not log reminder label text, precise GPS coordinates, or any other personally identifiable information. Analytics events contain only non-identifying usage signals.

Firebase Crashlytics may collect crash stack traces, device state at the time of a crash, and the app version. This information is used solely to diagnose and fix technical issues.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Subscriptions and payments — Google Play Billing

ThithiAlert offers an optional Premium subscription purchased through Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive, process, or store your payment card details. The only information we receive from Google is a token confirming whether your subscription is active.

Google Play's privacy practices are described at: Google Payments Privacy Notice

5. Remote configuration data

The app periodically downloads a festival calendar file and app configuration from our GitHub-hosted data repository (3stech.github.io/apkdata/thithialert/). These are static JSON files containing festival dates and app settings. No personal data is sent as part of these requests beyond standard HTTP metadata (IP address, date/time) that is handled by GitHub Pages infrastructure.

6. Feedback

If you use the in-app Feedback form, your message is sent as an email to 3s.technologies.chennai@gmail.com using your device's default email app. We do not automatically collect or store feedback messages — the standard email you compose is the only communication. You remain in full control of what you share.

7. Permissions

ThithiAlert requests the following Android permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to deliver panchangam reminders and festival alerts you have set up
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — to ensure reminders are delivered at the precise time you requested
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to reschedule your reminders if the device is restarted
  • INTERNET — to download the festival calendar and app configuration from our data repository, and to send analytics and crash data to Firebase

The app does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, files, or any other sensitive system resource.

8. Children's privacy

ThithiAlert is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

9. Data retention and deletion

Because all app data (reminders, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data in your Android Settings, or by uninstalling the app. Firebase Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention policies, which you can review at their privacy policy link above.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
